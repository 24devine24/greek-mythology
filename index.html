Greek mythology

Greek mythology, that is, the organized collection of myths from ancient Greece, developed over a very long period from Mycenaean civilization to Roman rule.

more information

The meeting between the Greeks and the Romans coincides with that of Greek mythology and Roman mythology: the first exerts a strong influence on the second, which is not reduced to it for all that.

Long after the disappearance of the Greek and Roman religions, Greek mythology was used as a subject of inspiration by artists, and continues to be so today.
